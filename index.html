About Me

Hi!

I'm Jake, a game design graduate from Brunel University London. I specialise in Unity development and designing active, real-time game mechanics with a primary focus on fun and game feel. My projects, both solo and collaborative, are made possible by a degree-level understanding of design theory and a wide range of practical skills such as programming and in-engine prototyping.

Like any game designer, I love playing games and found a world of bizarre, off-kilter and colourful adventures within the demo discs of my childhood gaming magazines, which continue to influence my work today. Now, having finished my degree, I'm excited to find my place in the industry, make some kick-ass games, and, ultimately, put smiles on faces, just like those wacky old games did for me!
