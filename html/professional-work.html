Professional Work


Doctor Who: Return to Earth - Nintendo Wii

2010

I was a level scripter on this project, working on the game through the early white-box stage to final release. I worked with the lead designer to establish tasks and then using the 3D level editor to implement various level and gameplay functionality, including doors, elevators, and platforms; enemy patrol paths; camera placement and fly-through paths. I was also given some freedom to do design and balancing work on the rolling ball mini-game, including the speeds and movement paths of hazards.

RESPONSIBILITIES: Level scripting | Playtesting
