Professional Work


Shikari X

2021

  • DEVELOPER: Solar Flare London
  • PLATFORM: PC
  • ENGINE: Unity
  • TEAM SIZE: 6

I am a programmer at Solar Flare London, a small startup company of Brunel University graduates working on Shikari X – a 3D, third-person, Metroidvania boss-rush title. In this game, players control a robot equipped with a sentient cannon, and must defeat 10 Shikari – rival robots piloting colossal mechs equipped with unique gun mods that the player can obtain by defeating them. I am working with a team of five others and we are aiming to complete a demo by October.

RESPONSIBILITIES: Programming | Design (3 Cs)


Doctor Who: Return to Earth

2010

  • DEVELOPER: Asylum Entertainment
  • PLATFORM: Nintendo Wii
  • ENGINE: In-house engine
  • TEAM SIZE: 20+

I was a level scripter on this project, working on the game through the early white-box stage to final release. I worked with the lead designer to establish tasks and then using the 3D level editor to implement various level and gameplay functionality, including doors, elevators, and platforms; enemy patrol paths; camera placement and fly-through paths. I was also given some freedom to do design and balancing work on the rolling ball mini-game, including the speeds and movement paths of hazards.

RESPONSIBILITIES: Level scripting | Playtesting
